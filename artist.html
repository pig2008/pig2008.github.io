Top Artists

Nina Kravis

One of the minimal, tech-house and house leading women DJs that produces and djs around the world mainly in Europe. Nina is a beautiful young Russian DJ she also sings on a couple of her songs she produces. In the early stages before Djing Nina did modelling, however at a party Nina tried Djing and has never look back producing top tracks such as “I’m Gonna Get You” & “Ghetto Kraviz” top tracks in year 2010 & 2012 played all over Europe in top European clubs wherever such goes she has a huge fan base.

Subb-an

Top DJ from the UK his sounds have been top notch for the last two years no one has come close to producing multiple top tracks in UK underground scene. He also produced so many quality remixes of other artist’s tracks; if you see a Sub Ann remix it will surely be worth a listen. His remix of anthea “distraction” was number 2 track of 2012. If most put the time and effort this dj does there would be so many quality songs out there. His standard has never fallen below standard he is for sure the UK’s number 1 DJ and producer of the last year.

Kerri Chandler

Top American DJ and commutes his sounds all over the world. He has been djing and producing songs since the 1990’s a mature man but has way of djing is so unique unlike the other top underground DJs. He mixes all the different music genre over multiple hour sets most likely 2 – 3 hours sets him DJs and never disappoints when you go and see him. One of three most in demand DJ’s for underground House.

East End Dubz

Another top underground DJ that doesn’t break out of his shell to be well known and also create his own unique minimal tech sounds. East End Dubs is the only UK DJ that just plays for the love of his music. His sounds are mainly unrealised and if there realised it is on his own music label and he also has one or two on another label. He plays his sounds in small clubs and rarely plays at big venues he just like playing his sounds to his loyal fans and followers. His top track “feelin” from his own music label.

Matt Tolfrey

He is a top UK producer that also DJ around the world he also has a weekly residents in Ibiza. His career started 10 years ago and not has global recognition for his music sounds. He is signed to multiple famous underground music labels and the entire top other DJs will have a few of his songs. He has team up with Heidi another top women DJ to promote his sounds more with a unique night which is a must for underground house raves. His top track is “for a while” (Huxley and matt tolfrey remix). From leftroom limited records.
